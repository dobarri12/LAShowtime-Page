Your Season Pass will grant you access to everything LA Showtime does this year! Whether you are a Role Player, a member of the Starting 5, or a bonafide MVP!

  • This Includes:
  • Special bonuses when joining giveaways
  • Better odds of winning tickets to Laker games
  • Access to Flash Challenges
  • Exclusive custom merchandise and physical cards
  • And much, much more!

As you interact with the community this year through collecting Laker moments, completing Flash Challenges, joining our IRL events, and gifting moments to the community, you will gain XP. This XP will earn you prizes, access to higher-level Flash Challenges and rewards, and more XP opportunities.

A list of challenges that grant XP can be found here, and a full view of the XP track and its rewards can be found here.

If you reach Level 100, you will be granted a veritable treasure trove of rewards, including a free Starting 5 pass for the following season and the ability to join a council that will help decide what goes into next year's Season Pass!
